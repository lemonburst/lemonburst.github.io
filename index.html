4 Pics 1 Word - Puzzle Game

When you open "4 Pics 1 Word - Puzzle Game", you'll be immersed in endless fun and challenge!

This word puzzle game features a simple and clear interface and easy-to-use controls, allowing you to guess a word through four pictures.

Every level will make you think and stimulate your brain, making you a smarter person. This game will never let you down!

How to play

➠ Look at four pictures.

➠ Guess a word that describes what all the pictures have in common.

➠ Select the correct letters from the given letters to form the word.

➠ Move on to the next level once you guess the correct word.

➠ You can use the hint feature to help you.
